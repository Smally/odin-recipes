Bambal

Ingredients

  • dried chillies
  • shallots
  • garlic
  • ginger
  • belachan
  • tomato
  • dried prawns
  • oil
  • big onion
  • tamarind peste
  • sugar
  • salt
  • protein of choice, eg. prawns,chicken etc

Steps

  1. Deseed and soak dried chillies in hot water (wear gloves if necesary)
  2. Blend together the chilies,shallots,garlic,ginger, belachan, tomatoes and dried prawns
  3. In a pan, fry the blended peste in oil until the peste darkens and oil seperates from the peste
  4. Once the peste is ready, add in sugar, salt and tamarind peste to teste
  5. Slice big onions into rings
  6. Add onion rings into peste and cook until tender
  7. Add protein of choice and fry until the meat is cooked. If using seafood, do not cook for too long to maintain nice texture.
