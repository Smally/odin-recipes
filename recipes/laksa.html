Laksa Curry

Ingredients

  • coconut milk
  • curry powder
  • french beans
  • chicken thighs
  • Thai red curry paste
  • egg noodles
  • vegetable oil

Steps

  1. cut chicken into thin strips
  2. fry paste in oil
  3. curry powder
  4. add cocont milk and bring to the boil
  5. add chicken
  6. add beans
